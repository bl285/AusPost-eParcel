AusPost Account Number

This number is related to the Australia Business Credit Account.
1. Go to website :
https://eparcel.auspost.com.au/eParcel/common/auth/login.do

2. Login this account and access eparcel website, then you will see page as this sample. Click Administration at top-right and choose the View All Post Charge To Account.

3. You will see your personal details, similar like this. When you want to login the AusPost eParcel application, use this 7 digits number as AusPost Account Number.
